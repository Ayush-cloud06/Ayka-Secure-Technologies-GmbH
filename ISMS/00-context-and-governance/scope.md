# Scope of the Information Security Management System (ISMS)

**Organization:** Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH  
**Location:** Stuttgart, Germany  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Approved by:** Managing Director  
**Effective Date:** 21-02-2026  

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the scope and boundaries of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) of Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001.

The ISMS is established to ensure the:

- Confidentiality of information  
- Integrity of information  
- Availability of information  

processed, stored, or transmitted by the organization.

---

## 2. Organizational Scope

The ISMS applies to:

- All employees  
- Contractors  
- Executive management  
- Interns and temporary staff  
- Third-party personnel with authorized system access  

The ISMS covers all departments, including:

- Executive Management  
- Engineering  
- Security Operations  
- Compliance  
- Sales & Customer Success  
- IT Support & Administration  

The ISMS governs all business processes related to:

- Design and development of the SaaS platform  
- Operation of cloud infrastructure  
- Delivery of compliance services  
- Customer onboarding and support  
- Internal administrative processes  

---

## 3. Technical and System Scope

The ISMS covers the following systems and environments:

- Cloud infrastructure hosted in Amazon Web Services (AWS)  
- AWS Organization structure and associated member accounts  
- Cloud-based SaaS application platform  
- Infrastructure-as-Code configurations (Terraform)  
- CI/CD pipelines and development environments  
- Logging, monitoring, and SIEM systems  
- Backup and disaster recovery systems  
- Internal IT systems used for business operations  

---

## 4. Information Assets in Scope

The ISMS applies to the following categories of information:

- Customer data processed by the SaaS platform  
- Compliance documentation and audit evidence  
- Risk management records  
- Internal company documentation  
- Employee and HR records  
- Financial and contractual information  
- Security logs and monitoring data  

---

## 5. Geographic Scope

- Primary operational location: Stuttgart, Germany  
- Remote work locations of authorized employees  
- Cloud infrastructure operating within approved AWS regions  

Approved cloud regions are defined in internal cloud governance policies.

---

## 6. Interfaces and Dependencies

The ISMS acknowledges dependencies on:

- Amazon Web Services (cloud infrastructure provider)  
- External SaaS tools supporting operations  
- Third-party suppliers and service providers  
- Customers integrating with Aykaâ€™s platform  

Supplier relationships are governed by contractual security requirements.

---

## 7. Exclusions

The following are excluded from the ISMS scope:

- Personal systems not used for company business  
- Customer-managed infrastructure not operated by Ayka  
- Third-party systems outside contractual or operational control  

Any expansion of services or infrastructure will trigger a scope review.

---

## 8. Scope Review

The ISMS scope is reviewed:

- Annually  
- Upon significant organizational change  
- Upon major technical architecture change  
- Following mergers, acquisitions, or new service lines
