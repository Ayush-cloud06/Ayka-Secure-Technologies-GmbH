# Information Security Policy

**Organization:** Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH  
**Location:** Stuttgart, Germany  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Approved by:** Managing Director  
**Effective Date:** 01-03-2026  
**Policy Owner:** Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)  

---

## 1. Purpose

Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets in order to:

- Maintain customer trust  
- Meet regulatory and contractual obligations  
- Protect company reputation  
- Ensure operational resilience  
- Support sustainable business growth  

This Information Security Policy establishes the foundation of the organizationâ€™s Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All employees  
- Contractors and temporary staff  
- Executive management  
- Third-party personnel with authorized access  
- All information systems within the defined ISMS scope  

All personnel are required to comply with this policy and supporting procedures.

---

## 3. Security Objectives

Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH establishes measurable information security objectives that are aligned with business strategy.

These objectives include:

- Maintaining ISO/IEC 27001 alignment  
- Ensuring 100% MFA enforcement for privileged access  
- Ensuring timely remediation of identified vulnerabilities  
- Maintaining secure logging and monitoring of cloud environments  
- Ensuring regular access reviews and least-privilege enforcement  
- Maintaining documented risk management processes  

Security objectives are reviewed annually during management review meetings.

---

## 4. Governance and Responsibilities

Information security is a management responsibility.

- Executive Management provides strategic direction and oversight.  
- The CISO is responsible for maintaining and improving the ISMS.  
- Department heads are responsible for implementing security controls within their areas.  
- All personnel are responsible for protecting information assets.  

Non-compliance with security requirements may result in disciplinary action.

---

## 5. Risk Management

Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH applies a structured risk management process to:

- Identify threats and vulnerabilities  
- Assess risk likelihood and impact  
- Determine appropriate risk treatment  
- Monitor and review residual risks  

Risk assessments are conducted at least annually or upon significant change.

---

## 6. Compliance Obligations

The organization commits to complying with:

- Applicable laws and regulations (including GDPR)  
- Contractual security obligations  
- Industry-specific compliance requirements  
- Internal policies and procedures  

Non-compliance is investigated and addressed through corrective action procedures.

---

## 7. Continuous Improvement

The ISMS is subject to continuous improvement through:

- Risk assessment reviews  
- Internal audits  
- Monitoring and measurement activities  
- Management reviews  
- Corrective and preventive actions  

The organization commits to maintaining and improving the effectiveness of the ISMS over time.

---

## 8. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed:

- Annually  
- Upon significant organizational or technical change  
- Following major incidents  
- Upon regulatory updates  

Approved revisions are communicated to all personnel.
