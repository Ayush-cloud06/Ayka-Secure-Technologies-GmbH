# Training and Awareness Program

Organization: Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH  
Location: Stuttgart, Germany  
Last Updated: 20-02-2026  
Owner: CISO

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the information security training and awareness program of Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH.

The objective is to ensure that all personnel:
- Understand their security responsibilities
- Are aware of current threats
- Are competent to perform their assigned roles securely
- Comply with ISO/IEC 27001 requirements

---

## 2. Scope

This program applies to:
- All full-time employees
- Contractors
- Interns
- Third-party personnel with system access

---

## 3. Training Categories

### 3.1 Mandatory Baseline Training (All Personnel)

Frequency: Upon onboarding + Annual refresh

Topics:
- Information Security Fundamentals
- Password & MFA Requirements
- Phishing & Social Engineering
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Incident Reporting Procedure
- GDPR Awareness
- Remote Work Security

Completion is required before access to production systems is granted.

---

### 3.2 Role-Based Training

Additional training is assigned based on primary role.

#### Executive (CEO, Product, Sales)
- Business continuity awareness
- Data protection obligations
- Regulatory responsibilities
- Crisis communication basics

#### Engineering (Cloud, Backend, Frontend)
- Secure coding practices
- Cloud security fundamentals
- IAM least privilege principles
- Infrastructure-as-Code security
- CI/CD security practices

#### Security & Compliance
- ISO/IEC 27001 controls
- Risk assessment methodology
- Incident response procedures
- Evidence collection & audit preparation
- GDPR compliance operations

#### IT & Support
- Asset management procedures
- Endpoint security controls
- Access provisioning/deprovisioning
- Backup & recovery procedures

---

## 4. Awareness Activities

The organization conducts:

- Quarterly phishing simulations
- Annual incident response tabletop exercise
- Security newsletter (quarterly)
- Policy change briefings (as required)

---

## 5. Training Records

Training completion records are maintained by HR & Administration.

Records include:
- Employee ID
- Training name
- Completion date
- Certification (if applicable)

Training evidence is retained for audit purposes.

---

## 6. Competency Evaluation

Competency is evaluated through:

- Training completion tracking
- Role performance reviews
- Incident handling assessments
- Technical skill verification (where applicable)

Additional training is assigned if competency gaps are identified.

---

## 7. Continuous Improvement

The training program is reviewed annually by the CISO.

Updates are made based on:
- Risk assessment results
- Incident trends
- Regulatory changes
- Organizational growth
