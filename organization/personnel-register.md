1. Overview

This personnel register documents all employees and formally assigned roles within Ayka Secure Technologies GmbH.

This register supports:

- ISO/IEC 27001 governance requirements
- Access control management
- Training and awareness tracking
- Role-based responsibility assignment
- Audit traceability
- Identity and access provisioning

All personnel are subject to contractual confidentiality obligations and mandatory security awareness training.

Access rights are provisioned based on formally assigned roles and least privilege principles.

Employee identifiers are used for audit references, access reviews, and security records.
